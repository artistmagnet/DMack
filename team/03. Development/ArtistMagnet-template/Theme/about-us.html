Artist Magnet - the theatre revolution...

Mission

Developing an online social network that allows you to build, print, store and share customized, professional, hyperlinking online Resumes you can actually use, instantly! Enabling you to be seen by the Industry in new ways that are beneficial to your professional career!

Company Overview

Artist Magnet - connecting Artists through Shows and Venues everywhere...

Description

AM members will have the opportunity and incentive to invite their friends and colleagues to join them in building the network in order to expand their Resume connectivity.

AM members will be able to more easily and efficiently search each other's Resumes, keep up-to-date with each other's work, and connect to a streamlined, virtual, global Theatre world.

As new theatrical projects are constantly being developed, and new artists are continuously entering the field and updating their Resumes, AM will maintain sustainability over the long-term.

AM will be designed with the ability to expand to music, dance, visual arts, multimedia, film, and other entertainment industries over the long-term.

Ultimately, AM will launch the Theatre world into the 21st century, setting the industry standard as a preferred source for comprehensive, professional artistic data worldwide.
