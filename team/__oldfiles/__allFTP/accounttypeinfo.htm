* Select an ARTIST account if you are an actor, director, playwright, dramaturg, designer, dancer, musician, etc.

* Select a PRODUCTION/MANAGEMENT account if you are a producer, stage manager, production manager, technical director, etc.

* Select a REPRESENTATIVE account if you are a manager, agent, casting director, legal representative, etc.

* Select a FAN account if you are a theatre patron, audience member, fan of an artist, production or venue, all-around theatre supporter, etc.

* Select a COMPANY account if you are a production company, theatre company, foundation, arts related organization, arts service provider, advertising company, etc.

* Only Artist and Production/Management accounts allow you to build your own customized hyperlinking resumes

* Are you associated with more than one category? Don't worry! Simply choose the one that most primarily relates to you; you will be able to build as many different hyperlinking resumes as you need in either the Artist or Production/Management categories regardless of which of those two account types you select during sign-up...
