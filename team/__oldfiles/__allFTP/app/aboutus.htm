About Artist Magnet:

Artist Magnet is the theatre revolution, connecting artists through productions and venues everywhere. The concept was developed out of a desire to see the theatre world enter the 21st century, where instead of Artists lugging around piles of paper resumes stapled to headshots, mailing and handing them out to hundreds of casting directors, managers, agents and producers to be put in a filing cabinet and in many cases never to be seen again Artists could simply mention their name and Artist Magnet, and anyone could go online and not only instantly find, print and share the Artists updated professional resumes, but also click on any past, present or upcoming productions they were involved with, venues they worked in or Directors they worked with, and be instantly forwarded to that show or venue page, displaying links to the resumes of every Artist that worked on that production, links to the pages of every show performed in that venue, and links to the resumes of every Director they worked with. We are only at the very first step and there is a long way to go with Artist Magnets network-in-development, but it is important that the hyperlinking resumes remain FREE for all Artists; production companies, agents, casting directors and producers will pay to get access to the Artists resumes, instead of the standard practice of Artists paying for the privilege of putting their resumes on producers websites. And theatre resumes are only the beginning; ultimately we will expand our services to every field of Artistry everywhere in the world. We hope you join us in this journey by contributing to the development of this site at PayPal or simply sending a message to info@artistmagnet.com

Thank you for your support and we look forward to seeing you soon on Artist Magnet!

Peace,

David Mack Founder/CEO Artist Magnet

About David Mack:



A graduate of California Institute of the Arts (MFA, Theatre) and Cornell University (BA, Theatre), David has been active in theatre for over 15 years in Los Angeles, New York, Europe and the Caribbean as a performer, director, and producer. David is producer of "Chocolate City," an intercultural showcase connecting Southern California artists of color to the Industry and Chairman of LA Supervisor Mark Ridley-Thomas' Empowerment Congress Arts & Culture Committee. David is also a member of the Board of Directors of the Mutineer Theatre Company and the Managing Director of Watts Village Theater Company. David is the Founder & CEO of Artist Magnet.
